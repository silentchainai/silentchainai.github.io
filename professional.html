Offensive Security

Advanced capabilities designed for penetration testers, red teamers, and bug bounty hunters.

⚡

Phase 2 Active Verification

AI generates targeted payloads to confirm findings with real exploitation attempts. Moves beyond passive analysis to proof-of-exploitation evidence.

WAF Detection & Evasion

Automatically fingerprint 25+ WAF vendors including Cloudflare, Akamai, ModSecurity, AWS WAF, and Imperva. AI generates bypass payloads tailored to each WAF.

Out-of-Band Testing

Detect blind vulnerabilities with OOB callbacks. Test for blind XSS, SSRF, XXE, RFI, and blind SQL injection that traditional scanners miss.

7 AI Providers

Cross-Product Correlation

Findings correlated with SILENTCHAIN Enterprise and Sn1per results via the RAG engine. Automatic severity escalation when multiple tools agree.
