About SILENTCHAIN AI

The AI-powered product division of Sn1perSecurity LLC, building the next generation of offensive security tools.

Who We Are

SILENTCHAIN AI is the AI-powered product division of Sn1perSecurity LLC, a company built by security professionals for security professionals. We develop offensive security tools that combine advanced language models with real-world vulnerability intelligence to deliver results that practitioners can trust.

Our products serve penetration testers, bug bounty hunters, red teams, and application security engineers who need AI that understands the nuances of real-world exploitation — not just pattern matching against training data.

Our Mission

Make AI-driven security testing reliable, private, and accessible. Generic LLMs hallucinate vulnerabilities. We solve this by grounding every AI analysis in a curated knowledge base of 80,000+ security documents — real exploits, CWE definitions, CVE data, and verified scan results.

We believe security tools should respect your data. Every SILENTCHAIN product supports fully local AI processing via Ollama, meaning your target data and source code never leave your machine. No cloud dependencies required.

We believe in open source as a foundation. SILENTCHAIN Community Edition is free and open source, giving every security professional access to AI-powered vulnerability detection without barriers.
